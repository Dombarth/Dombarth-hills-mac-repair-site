# Warranty & Terms (NSW aligned)
- **New parts:** 12‑month warranty (defects only).
- **Used/refurb/aftermarket:** 30‑day warranty (shortest allowed under ACL for used parts).
- Excludes accidental damage, liquid, household electrical faults, power spikes/surges/brownouts, overloading USB hubs/docks, charging phones from computer USB, third‑party accessories, and unrelated faults.  
- Australian Consumer Law rights apply in addition to these terms.
