# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** February 2025  
 

This Privacy Policy explains how Hills District Mac Repair ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

### 1.1 Contact Information
- Name
- Phone number
- Email address
- Physical address (for pickup/delivery services)

### 1.2 Device Information
- Mac model and serial number
- Description of issues or symptoms
- Login credentials (if required for repair, stored securely and deleted after service)

### 1.3 Payment Information
- Payment method details (processed securely through third-party payment providers)
- Transaction records

### 1.4 Communication Records
- Emails, SMS, and phone call records related to your repair
- Repair notes and service history

---

## 2. How We Collect Information

We collect personal information:
- Directly from you when you contact us, book a repair, or drop off a device
- Through our website contact forms and booking system
- Via phone calls, SMS, or email correspondence
- From third-party referrals (with your consent)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:
- Provide Mac repair and diagnostic services
- Communicate with you about your repair status
- Send quotes, invoices, and receipts
- Process payments
- Provide warranty support
- Respond to your enquiries
- Improve our services
- Comply with legal obligations

We will **not** use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

---

## 4. How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from:
- Misuse, interference, and loss
- Unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure

**Security measures include:**
- Secure storage of physical records
- Password-protected digital systems
- Limited staff access to personal information
- Secure deletion of login credentials after repair completion
- Encrypted communication where possible

---

## 5. Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your personal information to:
- Parts suppliers (device model only, no personal details)
- Payment processors (for transaction processing)
- Courier services (for pickup/delivery, address only)
- Legal authorities (if required by law)

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 8. Your Rights Under Australian Privacy Law

Under the Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to:
- **Access** your personal information held by us
- **Correct** inaccurate or outdated information
- **Request deletion** of your information (subject to legal requirements)
- **Opt out** of marketing communications
- **Complain** about privacy breaches

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

---

## 9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for:
- **Repair records:** 7 years (for warranty and legal purposes)
- **Contact information:** Until you request deletion
- **Payment records:** As required by Australian tax law

After the retention period, information is securely deleted or de-identified.

## 10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.
